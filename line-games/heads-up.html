How to Play

Hold the phone on your forehead, screen facing the group. They give clues.

Tilt phone DOWN (toward chin) = ✅ Correct
Tilt phone UP (toward forehead) = ⏭ Pass

No tilt? Tap the top half (✅) or bottom half (⏭) of the screen instead.
