Stress-Free Trip Logistics

Everything below is pre-arranged and paid for. No one needs to worry about logistics — just show up and enjoy. This is the plan to make sure families with young kids have zero stress from the moment they land.

🚌
Airport Transportation — Both Ways
Private shuttle from MCO → hotel on arrival day. Return shuttle on departure day. Hotel-move day: arranged transport between Disney and Universal area. Zero rental cars needed.
🍼
Baby Supplies Delivered to Hotel
Diapers, wipes, formula, baby food, and snacks ordered via Amazon/Walmart and delivered to the hotel before the group arrives. No store runs, no hauling through airports.
🛴
Strollers — Delivered & Donated
High-end strollers (1 UPPAbaby double + singles) ordered online, delivered to hotel before arrival. After the trip, donated locally. No checking strollers on planes.
⭐
VIP Tour Guides — Skip All Lines
Disney VIP Experience guides for both VIP groups + Universal VIP guides for all 3 Universal groups. Guides handle all in-park logistics — no strategy needed. Skip standby, maximize every hour.
⌚
MagicBand+ Pre-Linked & Charging Enabled
All 20 MagicBand+ wristbands ordered and linked to Disney accounts before the trip. Hotel key, Lightning Lane, PhotoPass, and park entry — all on the wrist. Just tap and go.
⚠️ Pre-Arrival To-Do — Room Charging Setup:
At Disney check-in, enable room charging on every parent's MagicBand+ — all linked to Trevor's master folio with no spending limit. Parents tap their band anywhere on Disney property (parks, restaurants, gift shops, hotel) and it goes straight to the room bill. Kids do not need charging enabled. Remind all parents this is for their group's extras & souvenirs only — all meals, tickets, and necessities are already covered separately.
🗝️
Universal Room Key — Charging Enabled
At Portofino Bay check-in, activate room charging on every parent's room key card — all tied to Trevor's master account with no spending limit. Works at all Universal Orlando restaurants, merchandise shops, and CityWalk on-property venues. Same principle as Disney: parents charge freely, Trevor settles at checkout. Kids' cards should have charging disabled at the front desk.
⚠️ Pre-Arrival To-Do: Call Portofino Bay before arrival to set up the master folio and confirm room charging can be activated at check-in for all rooms under the group booking. Ask for a group coordinator — large parties often get a dedicated contact.
⚡
Lightning Lane — Pre-Planned
Lightning Lane Multi Pass purchased for every park day. Individual Lightning Lane reserved at 7 AM each morning (resort advantage). Disney app pre-loaded and ready at 6:55 AM. Top rides locked in before walking through the gate.
🍽️
All Dining Pre-Reserved
Every table-service restaurant booked the moment the 60-day window opens. No in-park scrambling for tables, no waiting in line to eat. Sit down, order, enjoy.
🌙
Nightly Group Check-In
Quick 5-minute family huddle each evening: next day's park, VIP group splits, meet time, and any updates. Everyone knows the plan. No morning chaos, no confusion.
📱
Park Reservations + Tickets Done
All Disney park reservations secured. All tickets linked to accounts. All Universal tickets purchased and tied to the hotel Express Pass. Walk up and walk in — nothing to figure out at the gate.
💆
Built-In Recovery Days
Pool days, rest days, and a free/flex day built into the schedule. Families with young kids can opt out of a park day and do resort pool time anytime. No pressure to do everything every day.
📱
Group Communication Plan
Everyone uses their phones. One shared group chat (WhatsApp or GroupMe) for all adults — used for daily plans, VIP group locations, and real-time updates. A second chat for just the VIP group leads. Nightly check-in each evening confirms next day's schedule so mornings are calm.
🏷️
Kids' Safety — IDs & Trackers
Tracker tags (Apple AirTag or Tile) on each young child's shoes or inside jacket pocket. Emergency contact info written on each child's wrist with Sharpie each morning (parent phone number) — Disney cast members are trained to assist separated children. VIP guides are also responsible for group headcounts.
🧳
Car Seats — Each Family Brings Their Own
Each family with young children brings their own travel/foldable car seat through the airport. This avoids airport rental hassle and ensures legal compliance in all Uber/shuttle rides. Lightweight travel car seats (WAYB Pico, Cosco Scenera Next) are easiest to gate-check.
⛈️
Rain Contingency — Arcade Day
Florida afternoon storms happen even in November. If it rains mid-park day, the backup plan is an arcade day — head to Andretti Indoor Karting & Games or DEZERLAND Park (both covered, all-weather). The day's lunch at Texas de Brazil works perfectly as a same-day pairing. Rain ponchos in the grocery delivery ensure a smooth transition.
💊
Medications & Motion Sickness
Non-prescription medications ordered via Amazon/Instacart and delivered to the hotel with the grocery order: Dramamine (simulator rides cause nausea even in non-coaster people), children's Tylenol & ibuprofen, adult Tylenol, antacids, allergy meds, Band-Aids, blister pads, and Benadryl. No pharmacy run needed mid-trip.

Notes & Tips

Lightning Lane — Large Group

Book Lightning Lane Multi Pass at 7 AM on park day. With 20+ people split across 2 VIP groups, coordinate who books what. Individual Lightning Lane for top rides sells out by 8–9 AM.

VIP Split Strategy

VIP 1 (kids-heavy) and VIP 2 (teens/adults) tackle different rides simultaneously, then merge for meals and shows. Maximizes ride count for everyone while keeping pace age-appropriate.

Rope Drop

Arrive 30–45 min before park open. Split VIP groups immediately — big-ride group sprints to top thrill rides, kids group heads to their priority list. Lines are 15–30 min at open vs 90 min by 10 AM.

Florida Weather — Fall

October/November: afternoon storms are common but shorter than summer. Plan indoor rides or sit-down lunch 1–3 PM. Pack ponchos — umbrellas are awkward in crowds.

Dining — Large Group

Reservations open 60 days out. Character dining and Be Our Guest book immediately. Tables for 20+ are rare — expect to split across nearby tables. Confirm when reserving.

Strollers & Baby Gear

Buying a cheap stroller at Orlando Target/Walmart near Disney may be easier than flying with one. Budget extra checked bag per family group for the return flight haul.

Hotel Move (Universal Days)

If moving resorts for Universal days, do it on a light day or mid-trip. Use the hotel-move day type in the builder. Universal on-site hotels offer Express Pass perks — worth pricing out.

Epic Universe (2025+)

By 2029 there will be 3 Universal parks: USF, Islands of Adventure, and Epic Universe (opens 2025). EU includes Harry Potter worlds, Nintendo, and more — plan at least 1 EU day.
